Nation building campaign

A month long campaign to inspire and engage youth towards Nation Building activities.

The Story behind

"The youth must believe in the power of goodness and learn to take proactive action in the right direction. Taking inspiration from Swami Vivekananda and Subhash Chandra Bose, we celebrate 12th January and 23rd January (the birth anniversary of two of the greatest heroes of our nation) as two important days during this campaign. It is important for good activities to make noise. Negativity usually has its own way to spread, it creates loss of trust and despondency in the society in general."

The Process

We at Samskruti Foundation run a month long campaign called Vivek band do good be good campaign every year. Each year we take a different theme and involve the youth to take a pledge to Be good and indulge into an activity of nation building as Do Good.

In the last few years the campaign included activities like organising social entrepreneurship program in colleges across the country, a blood & eye donation camp, swachhata drive, helping underprivileged people by various means, health and hygiene promotion, Gift a flag for building nation's pride, Yoga for youth drive etc.

Thousands of students from various backgrounds get involved into this do good be good campaign and become the ambassador of goodness. The campaign aims to make youth believe in their power of action and inculcate the sense of duty towards Nation.
