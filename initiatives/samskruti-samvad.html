Samskruti Samvad

A forum for intellectual debates on our culture and its ancient practises and its relavance on present times

The Story behind

"The spiritual and philosophical underpinnings of our culture explores all aspects of life. It signifies the essence of human life is centric to the wellbeing of society.Thus our identity should reflect the richness of our culture that we follow.

There is growing need in our society to nurture a modern culture inspired from our past and the everlasting value system that is still relevant in todayâ€™s world.We believe modernisation is not westernisation.

Samskruti Samvad is an initiative aimed to nurture the understanding of bharatiya culture, discuss and debate on the value system.

The Process

"To build deeper understanding of our cultural ethos & to instill indigenous pride ,we at Samskruti Foundation initiate discussion forums at various platforms.

Samvad events operate as a dialogue amongst the participants, to find a modern relevance of various cultural practices that have nationalism at the core of their proposition."
