Our Vision

Culture is the bedrock of any civilisation. The only civilisation that stood the test of time is the Saraswati ñ Sindhu civilisation of bhArat ñ i.e., India - sustained all along by the indefatigable sanAtana Hindu Dharma.

The spiritual underpinnings of the bhAratEya culture directed all aspects of life of our society, reflected through the four pillars of human existence: dharma (ethics and values based conduct, existence), artha (material and economic needs / goals of the mind and body), kAma (fulfilment of wants and material enjoyment) and mOksha (attainment of the Supreme Bliss). All activities had their base in dharma with the attainment of mOksha being the goal for the same.

Over the ages, this framework put this civilisation at the forefront of global human development. World-class universities were setup and gave refuge to learners from world over. Trade and commerce flourished making bhArat the powerhouse of global economic development (as highlighted in the work of Angus Maddison on The World Economy: A Millennial Perspective. Literature and arts flourished. Happiness - within and without - abounded. The society was at its zenith.

Things changed - for the worse - with time over the past millennium and specifically so, over the past three hundred years! The civilisation considerably weakened, reduced in geography - and in all respects - to India. This downslide cannot be allowed any further! Where better to start, and what better to take inspiration from, than the eons young bhAratEya samskRuti, to rejuvenate the society and direct it towards the pinnacle of glory!

Samskruti Foundation's vision is to accomplish the same.

Our Mission

Samskruti Foundation stands and works for enriching our identity based on strength of our culture. The Foundation protects, preserves and propagates cultural values based on our Dharmic principles on many a fora to reach to different strata of our society.
