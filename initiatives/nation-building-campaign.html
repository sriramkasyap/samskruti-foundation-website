Nation building campaign

A month long campaign to inspire and engage youth towards Nation Building activities.

The Story behind

It is important for good activities to make noise. Negativity usually has its own way to spread, it creates loss of trust and despondency in the society in general.

The youth must believe in the power of goodness and learn to take proactive action in the right direction. For this purpose, we at Samskruti Foundation run a month long campaign called Vivek band do good be good campaign every year.

Taking inspiration from Swami Vivekananda and Subhash Chandra Bose, we celebrate 12th January and 23rd January (the birth anniversary of two of the greatest heroes of our nation) as two important days during this campaign.

Every year we take a different theme and involve the youth to take a pledge to 'Be good and indulge into an activity of nation building as Do Good.

In the year 2021 the theme is to become a social entrepreneur and to move from being a problem talker to solution seeker. Thousands of students and hundreds of colleges from all over India have participated in the workshops on Social entrepreneurship and are inspired to become a solution provider.

Many of them have given a thought to the idea of social entrepreneurship and becoming a job provider rather than being a job seeker. They have propelled themselves into finding out the solution of a local problem and create a business proposition out of that.

In the last few years the campaign included activities like organising a blood donation or eye donation camp, involving into swachhata drive, helping underprivileged people by various means, health and hygiene promotion drive etc.

Thousands of students from various backgrounds get involved into this do good be good campaign and become the ambassador of goodness.

The campaign aims to make youth believe in their power of action and inculcate the sense of duty towards Nation.
