Tejaswini

Tejaswini aims to collaborate the elite women power of society to solve social problems by designing solutions that are rooted in Bharatiya culture

The Story behind

Tejaswini is another initiative of foundation to address women centric issues and challenges faced by them in the current times based on our time tested methods. Women played a major role in shaping and sustaining our culture. Our age old dharma regards and respects the role of woman in our society with reverence. Present day women are facing many a challenges at domestic and professional front in competitive environment to stand on their own. They need to be strengthened to build a strong family, society and thus nation. They can derive strength with knowledge of our time tested culture. Present day education system is not giving them a good and proper orientation of our culture to derive the required strength to face the tough times

The Process

Elite women in the society as experts in our value system are grouped under this initiative to address specific problems of women in the present society.

  • Regular brainstorming sessions within the women elite to identify key problem areas and to design sustainable solutions that are rooted in our culture

  • Conduct seminars or town hall meetings with eminent women personalities of the society on a quarterly/half-yearly basis

  • Help the foundation in the execution of the yearly event of Rakhi for soldiers by networking all the girl students from different engineering colleges
