About Us

Culture is the bedrock of any civilisation. The only civilisation that stood the test of time is the Saraswati ñ Sindhu civilisation of bhArat ñ i.e., India - sustained all along by the indefatigable sanAtana Hindu Dharma.

Over the ages, this framework put this civilisation at the forefront of global human development. World-class universities were setup and gave refuge to learners from world over. Trade and commerce flourished making bhArat the powerhouse of global economic development (as highlighted in the work of Angus Maddison on The World Economy: A Millennial Perspective. Literature and arts flourished. Happiness - within and without - abounded. The society was at its zenith.

Samskruti Foundation's vision is to accomplish the same.
